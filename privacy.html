Privacy Policy

Effective July 5, 2026 · WealthHQ is operated by EdConsult LLC ("we," "us")

WealthHQ is a personal financial management application. This policy explains what information the application handles, why, and the choices you have. The short version: your financial data exists in WealthHQ solely so that you can see and manage it. We do not sell it, advertise against it, or share it with anyone except the service providers required to run the application.

Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address and a password (stored only as a cryptographic hash), used to sign you in.
  • Financial information you enter: assets, liabilities, properties, businesses, goals, documents, and related records you choose to add.
  • Financial information from linked accounts: if you connect a bank or brokerage, we receive account names, types, and balances through Plaid (see below). We never see or store your bank username or password.
  • Usage records: an audit log of create, update, and delete actions in your account, kept for security.

Bank connections through Plaid

WealthHQ uses Plaid Inc. to connect your financial institutions. When you link an account, you provide your credentials directly to Plaid — never to WealthHQ — and you authorize Plaid to retrieve your account information and share it with this application. Plaid's handling of your information is described in the Plaid End User Privacy Policy. By connecting an account, you grant WealthHQ and Plaid the rights described there. You can disconnect any institution at any time from Settings, which revokes the connection at Plaid, and you can also manage connections at my.plaid.com.

Consent

We collect and process your information only with your consent: creating an account constitutes consent to process the information described here for the purposes described here; entering financial records is your direction to store them; and linking a bank account requires your explicit, per-institution authorization through Plaid Link. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting records, disconnecting institutions, or requesting account deletion.

How we use information

Solely to operate the application for you: displaying your net worth and reports, syncing balances you've connected, generating insights you request from the AI advisor, and securing the service. We do not sell personal information, share it for advertising, or use it for any purpose unrelated to providing WealthHQ to you.

Service providers

Your data is processed by a small set of infrastructure providers under their own security and privacy obligations: Supabase (database, authentication, and document storage), Netlify (application hosting and serverless functions), Plaid (bank connectivity, only if you link accounts), and Anthropic (AI advisor responses, only when you ask the advisor a question — queries include relevant financial figures from your ledger and are used to generate the response, not for advertising).

Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Access to your records is isolated per household by database row-level security. Sign-in supports time-based one-time-password multi-factor authentication. Bank access tokens are stored server-side in a table no browser session can read. Administrative access to production systems requires multi-factor authentication. Details are documented in our Information Security Policy, available on request.

Data retention and deletion

  • Your control, immediately: you can permanently delete any record, document, or bank connection from within the application at any time. Disconnecting a bank revokes its access token at Plaid.
  • Account deletion: on request, we delete your account and all household data — financial records, documents, logs, and Plaid tokens (with connections revoked at Plaid). Deletion is completed within 30 days of a verified request.
  • Backups: deleted data leaves encrypted database backups automatically as they expire on the provider's rolling schedule (up to 30 additional days).
  • Retention default: we keep data only as long as your account is active; we do not retain financial data for any secondary purpose. This policy is reviewed at least annually.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information. WealthHQ supports these directly: your data is visible and editable in the application, exportable to CSV from Reporting, and deletable as described above. For anything else, contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

Children

WealthHQ is not directed to children and may not be used by anyone under 18.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date above and note the change in the application.
